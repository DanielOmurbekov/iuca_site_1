Erasmus+ Staff Mobility

Erasmus+ Staff Mobility

ERASMUS + is an EU exchange programme which has been running since the mid-1980s. The Czech Republic has been participating since 1997. The mobilities are performed on the basis of bilateral agreements signed with partner universities. Financial support is provided by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports and the LLP ERASMUS+ headquarters in Brussels via Rectorate of Charles University (RCU).

ERASMUS + STAFF MOBILITY CALL (JANUARY- MARCH 2024) CLOSED

Call for Erasmus+ Staff Mobility

IS věda

An essential part of the application is uploading the Erasmus+ staff mobility agreement to the application and securing the signature of the superior and the signature of the partner receiving institution. Should you not have a signature from partner institution at disposal please submit the agreement with only your signature and your superior. In order to sucessfully submit the Erasmus mobility application it is necessary to change the status for "Submitted" in the online application by the deadline. Otherwise it will not be included into the evaluation process of the call.

Erasmus+ staff mobility agreement

Manual how to fill in Mobility Agreement

1. Staff Mobility For Teaching

Staff mobility for teaching can be performed in the member as well as non-member EU countries as follows: Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Iceland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Liechtenstein, Luxembourg, Malta, Malta, the Netherlands, North Macedonia, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Serbia, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Turkey and the United Kingdom.

The recommended length of the teaching mobility is 5 working days. In justified cases, it is possible to carry out a shorter stay of at least 2 consecutive working days, excluding travel. The maximum length of stay is 2 months. However, it is necessary for you to teach at least 8 teaching hours (for 2 - 5 working days). The minimum number of teaching hours for mobilities exceeding 5 working days is calculated as follows: number of working days x 1.6

BILATERAL AGREEMENTS

Agreements

FINANCIAL SUPPORT

The financial support is composed out of two parts, one to support the travel costs and the other for accommodation.

In the case of virtual mobility, there is no entitlement to financial support.

The means are calculated in the maximum expected amount. In case of exceeding, it is necessary to secure an additional source of funding at the relevant institute/workplace.

ERASMUS+ TEACHING MOBILITY PLATFORM

The platform https://teachingmobility.eu  serves as a database for searching for academic mobilities. On this page you can monitor the possibilities and the entire interface even without registration. To publish your own offers and address the academics, you need to register: 

1. Click Log in in the upper right corner
2. Click Log in with MyAcademicID
3. Charles University must appear in the line that pops up on the Login page (it will pop up when typing in the menu)
4. There is a verification and login via CAS
5. Subsequently, the registration is completed and you can complete a short questionnaire
6. In the My content tab, you can create your own offers
7. You can also address academics on published profiles

3. The combined stay (educational stay and training )

The combined stay combines an educational stay and training within one mobility, which can be carried out at one or more eligible institutions in the destination country.

4. The blended intensive program (BIP)

The blended intensive program (BIP) is a specific type of employee mobility, including a mandatory virtual component in combination with physical mobility to foreign institutions. This type of mobility is linked to a separate, pre-approved project application, involving at least three institutions from three program countries, which must be linked by interinstitutional agreements.

The length of the mobility (excluding travel days) is at least 5 and at the most 30 days of physical mobility and any non-zero number of days of the virtual mobility.
