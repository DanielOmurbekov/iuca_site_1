    Research

    FSV UK fully supports the socio-scientific research. The Faculty integrates and co-ordinates the research plan it has acquired with several departments of  the Faculty of Arts, Charles University. Within this framework, FSV UK has made a commitment to provide the professional public with a critical and scientifically-based reflection on the development of Czech society from a long-term perspective.

Experience

In this section, you can learn from other students' experiences and get inspired for your next study stay abroad. You will find videos and pictures both from FSV students, who spent a period abroad and from our short-time exchange students, who talk about their home university. 

Another interesting survey of other students' experiences can be found on Charles Abroad website (in Czech). Student Exchange feedback given by students of all CU faculties and from their stays all over the world (Europe, Asia, America, Australia).

If any program or destination attracts you, do not hesitate to contact the FSV International Office for further information.
